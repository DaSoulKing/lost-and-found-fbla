How it works

Three simple steps

Whether you found something or lost something, the process takes less than two minutes.

  1. 01

    Someone reports it

    A student or staff member finds an item and submits it here with a photo and description.

  2. 02

    You browse & find it

    Search the listings by keyword, category, or date to find your missing item.

  3. 03

    Verify & collect

    Answer a quick verification question to prove ownership, then pick up your item from the main office.
