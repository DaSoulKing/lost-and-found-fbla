How claiming works

  1. 1 Select the item you believe is yours from the list.
  2. 2 Answer a custom question about the item that only the true owner would know.
  3. 3 An admin reviews your answer and approves or follows up.
  4. 4 Collect your item from the main office with your student ID.

Privacy notice

Your verification answer and contact info are kept private. They are only visible to the admin team and are never posted publicly.
