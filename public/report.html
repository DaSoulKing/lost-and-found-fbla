Tips for a good report

  • A clear photo is the single most helpful thing you can add.
  • Note any brand names, colors, or unique marks including scratches, stickers, etc.
  • Be specific about where you found it. Eg; "cafeteria near the vending machines" is better than "inside."
  • Don't include personal info visible inside the item. That's for the admin to verify privately.

Where to drop it off

After submitting, please bring the item to the Main Office (Room 100). Open Monday–Friday, 7:30 AM – 4:00 PM.
